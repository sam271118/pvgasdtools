# Privacy Policy for PV GAS D Calc

**Last updated: April 2026**

PV GAS D built the PV GAS D Calc app as a Free app. This SERVICE is provided by PV GAS D at no cost and is intended for use as is.

**1. Information Collection and Use**
We completely respect your privacy. The PV GAS D Calc app **DOES NOT** collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data, user behavior, or device identifiers. 

**2. Permissions Details**
The app only requires the `INTERNET` permission to automatically check for new app version updates from our server. 

**3. Local Data Storage**
Any data, including calculation history or custom user-defined fuel properties, are stored locally on your own device and are never uploaded to any remote server or third party.

**4. Contact Us**
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
